Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the information you provide directly, such as your name and email address when creating an account, and the expense records you log, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We may also collect basic technical information, such as browser type, to help us maintain and improve the Service.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data you provide to operate the Service: authenticating your account, storing and displaying your expenses, and generating the summaries and reports you see within the app. We do not use your expense data for advertising, and we do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your account and expense data are stored securely in our database and are associated only with your account. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, but no method of electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may rely on third-party services for functions such as hosting, authentication, or infrastructure. These providers only process data as necessary to deliver their service to us and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes. We do not share your expense data with advertisers or data brokers.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, you can reach out to us through the contact details provided on your account page. We're happy to help clarify how your information is used.
